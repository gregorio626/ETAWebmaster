Electronic Media (1 Credit)

Students will use programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Flash in order to create art. Students in this class will learn about famous artists and their art styles in order to imitate them.

Graphic Design (1 Credit)

In this class, students learn about logo design, vectors, calligraphy, and much more. Students use programs such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to complete projects.

Relay For Life (Extracurricular Activity)

Mrs. Gentry is the sponsor of Relay For Life. This organization not only provides opportunities for service hours, but also to fight in the battle against cancer.

Technology Student Association - TSA (Extracurricular Activity)

Mrs. Gentry is the sponsor of ETA's TSA chapter. She is the person who consistently keeps TSA Chapter 20 an engaging and refreshing experience that motivates students to succeed.
