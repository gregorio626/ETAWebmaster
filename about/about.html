ABOUT ETA

The Engineering and Technologies Academy provides students with the individual attention they need to master the curriculum and reach their full potential.

Opportunities are provided for building teamwork, problem solving, communication and creativity skills.


The Academy also engages students in engineering processes used in the industry.


ETA has a fantastic staff that is committed to serving its students in the best possible fashion 100% of the time.

Six Pillars of ETA

1.

Knowledge

Students will develop an understanding of the engineering field of study for the particular course they are engaged in.

2.

Experience

Students will learn about and use the latest software or processes used in the engineering and technologies industry.

3.

Problem Solving

Students are challenged in a project-based learning environment by applying their knowledge and experience to create a solution.

4.

Planning

Students will be instructed in an integrated academic environment to help create and deliver a final project.

5.

Production

Students will develop working skills and become prepared for higher education opportunities.

6.

Opportunities

Student opportunities include: Scholarships, internships, college credits, youth leadership programs, community service activities, Technology Student Association membership and competitions.
